# Confidence Levels

Confidence levels communicate how strongly a canon entry is validated.

Levels:
- experimental: Newly observed or proposed; not yet validated across systems.
- provisional: Reviewed and accepted for limited use; validation ongoing.
- verified: Fully validated and required for all applicable systems.
- deprecated: No longer valid; replaced by a newer entry.

Rules:
- Every canon entry MUST declare one of the confidence levels above.
- A confidence change MUST be recorded in evolution/changelog.md.
- Deprecated entries MUST reference their replacement IDs.
